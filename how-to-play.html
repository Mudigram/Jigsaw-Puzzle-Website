How to Play Bear Puzzle

1. Start the Game

Click the "Start" button to begin the puzzle. Time begins as soon as you make your first move.

2. Solve the Puzzle

Arrange the pieces to form the bear picture. The faster you complete it, the better your score!

3. Submit Your Time

When finished, enter your name to save your best time on the leaderboard.

4. Share Your Score

Click the "Share" button to post your best time on Twitter and challenge your friends.
