About Bear Puzzle

Welcome to Bear Puzzle – a fun, interactive jigsaw puzzle game where you piece together adorable bear-themed images! This project was inspired by a love for both puzzles and creativity, offering players a delightful challenge while showcasing cute bear animations.

🧩 Our goal: To deliver an engaging, bear-themed puzzle experience while encouraging friendly competition through leaderboards and social sharing!

Features:

  • Bear-themed jigsaw puzzles 🐻
  • Leaderboard for top puzzle solvers 🏆
  • Player profiles to track your progress 👤
  • Share your best times on Twitter 📤

Whether you're here to break records or simply unwind, Bear Puzzle brings the fun. Thank you for playing!
